# E-Commerce Profitability Analysis

## Business Problem
Despite strong sales growth, the business experienced inconsistent profitability due to excessive discounting, low-margin products, and loss-making transactions. Limited visibility into the relationship between discounts, product categories, and profit performance made it difficult to optimize pricing and promotional strategies.

---

## Objective
Analyze how discounts, product categories, and regional sales impact profitability to identify key drivers of profit loss and opportunities for margin improvement.

---

## Key Insights
- Products with **0–10% discounts** achieved the highest profit margin at **30.05%**
- Discounts above **40%** resulted in negative profitability, with margins falling to **-27.01%**
- Approximately **13.37% of total orders** fell into the high-risk discount zone (>40%)
- **Body Care** was the highest-performing category, generating **$590.9K profit**
- **Home and Accessories** was the most loss-making category with **-$57.2K profit**

---

## Business Recommendations
- Maintain discounts below identified high-risk thresholds
- Reduce aggressive discounting in low-margin categories
- Prioritize promotions for high-profit product groups
- Monitor high-discount transactions to control profit leakage
- Implement targeted discount strategies instead of blanket discount campaigns
